Choose the Required Roles

Generator





The role responsible for selecting the types of resources or materials that need recycling within an organization or system.

Handler





The role tasked with managing and physically handling the collected recyclable materials, ensuring they are processed correctly.

Decision Maker





The role with the authority to make final decisions on recycling processes, strategies, and investments based on the recommendations from the Generator and Handler.
