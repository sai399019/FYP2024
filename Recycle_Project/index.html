About Us

The ‘Decision Maker Software for Waste Management System in Institutional Campus’ is a web application aimed to provide a digital way of disposing wastes in efficient ways around the institutional campus. The main three aspects of this website are:

  • Generator: An interactive console that keeps track of waste disposed of in every building of the campus. This aspect provides real-time data on waste generation rates, allowing for accurate monitoring and analysis.
  • Handler: Provides information on how to dispose of waste materials based on their type. This aspect offers guidance on proper waste segregation and disposal practices, ensuring compliance with regulations and maximizing recycling opportunities.
  • Ultimate Decision Maker: Offers information on customized waste treatment for different types of waste disposed of on campus. This aspect utilizes advanced algorithms to recommend tailored waste treatment solutions, optimizing resource utilization and minimizing environmental impact.

In today's increasingly eco-conscious world, institutions are under mounting pressure to adopt sustainable practices, particularly in waste management. The efficient management of waste within institutional campuses poses a significant challenge, necessitating innovative solutions. In response to this need, the development of a cutting-edge Decision Maker Software tailored specifically for waste management in institutional campus.

This software integrates advanced algorithms and data analytics to streamline the waste management process within institutional settings. By leveraging real-time data collection and analysis, it empowers decision-makers to make informed choices regarding waste disposal, recycling initiatives, and resource allocation.
